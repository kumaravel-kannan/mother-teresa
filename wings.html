Mother Teresa Forum - Membership

Mother Teresa Forum (MTF), the execution wing of Mother Teresa charitable Trust enrolls members
from all citizens of India both male, female or transgenders who is having the willingness to serve
irrespective of caste, creed, race, religion, region, political affiliation etc. are eligible to enroll as a
member. Membership is free of cost. Enrolled members can opt for positions in the MTF, i.e., Natio
nwide, statewide, District wide, Zonal wide (Taluk), Village level etc. depending on their area of inte
rest or time to spare. “Service to man kind is service to God” which is sacrilegious and should be han
dled with utmost care and dedication. Mother Teresa Forum expects honesty, sincerity, dedication in
the service rendered by our volunteers. We serve under the noble footsteps of the great Mother, Mother
Teresa. We expect all our activities transparent and with dedication. On enrolment as member, the volu
nteers can opt for positions in the prescribed formats. MTF board deserves the right to allow or reject the
nominations without assigning any reason.
